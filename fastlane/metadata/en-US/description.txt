See the current electricity price on your Apple Watch, iPhone, and iPad.
Whether you plan to charge your electric car or use household appliances, stay informed with the electricity price and plan your energy usage smartly.

- Complications for Apple Watch
- Widgets for iPhone and iPad
- Support for multiple currencies

Pro features:
- View Mode: Extend the graph to include all of tomorrow's prices.
- Price Limits: Adjust the limits for what you consider to be a high and low price.
- Price Adjustment: Adjust the price by adding VAT and fees to estimate what you actually pay for your electricity.
